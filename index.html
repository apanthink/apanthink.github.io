Game Modes

Memory Card Game

The classic memory matching game with a Chinese twist. Flip cards to find matching pairs of characters while learning their meanings and pronunciations.

  • Match pairs of Chinese characters
  • Learn character meanings
  • Hear authentic pronunciations
  • Progress through 12 categories

Idiom Challenge

Test your knowledge of Chinese idioms by filling in the missing characters. Learn the cultural context and meaning behind common Chinese expressions.

  • Complete famous Chinese idioms
  • Learn idiom meanings and usage
  • See example sentences
  • Understand cultural context
