Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2024

Jun Pan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Chinese Cards.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be educational and suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
